Unlocking Mobiles

Mobile phone unlocking refers to the process of removing carrier restrictions from a device, allowing it to be used with different network providers. When a phone is locked, it can only operate with the specific carrier it was purchased from, often as part of a contract or installment plan. Unlocking typically requires contacting the carrier for an unlock code or using third-party services, and the phone must meet certain conditions, like completing the contract or being fully paid off.

Water Damage

Mobile water damage repair involves restoring a phone that has been exposed to water or other liquids. The process generally includes disassembling the device, drying internal components, and cleaning any corrosion that may have occurred due to water contact. Technicians often use specialized tools like ultrasonic cleaners to remove debris and moisture. Water damage can lead to a variety of issues, including short circuits, malfunctioning buttons, and display problems, so repair may involve replacing damaged parts.
